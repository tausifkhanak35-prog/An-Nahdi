About An Nahdi

Our story, mission, and the values that drive us.

Our Story & Mission

An Nahdi Educational Trust was born from a simple, powerful belief: every child deserves access to quality education. Witnessing the stark educational disparities in India—driven by poverty, lack of infrastructure, and social barriers—we decided to act. Our mission is to provide free, high-quality, and holistic education to underprivileged children, empowering them to build a better future for themselves and their communities.

The Challenge We Address

Lack of access to proper schools and learning materials.

High dropout rates due to economic pressures.

Inadequate infrastructure and a shortage of trained educators.

Socio-cultural barriers that disproportionately hinder girls' education.

Our Vision

We envision an India where every child, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to receive an education that nurtures their mind, body, and character, enabling them to become confident, responsible, and future-ready individuals.

Our Values

  • Equity: We prioritize the most vulnerable.
  • Quality: High-standard, holistic learning.
  • Community: Working with communities.
  • Integrity: Transparency and accountability.
  • Innovation: Evolving to meet children's needs.
